Who I am

A Brit who went all in on Thailand

I'm Emily, originally from Surrey and now Bangkok through and through. I've been speaking Thai for over 12 years, and I make food, culture and travel content that gets Thai viewers laughing and international viewers booking flights.

My community trusts what I recommend because I actually live it: chasing the best street food, exploring provinces and festivals, and showing what daily life as a foreigner in Thailand is really like. I also co-run a Bangkok tattoo studio with my husband.
