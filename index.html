Who I am

A Brit who went all in on Thailand

I'm Emily — originally from Surrey, now Bangkok through and through. I've been speaking Thai for over 12 years, and I make the kind of food, culture and travel content that gets Thai audiences laughing and international audiences booking flights.

My community trusts my recommendations because I live it: hunting the best street food, exploring provinces and festivals, and showing what daily life as a foreigner in Thailand really looks like. I also co-run a Bangkok tattoo studio with my husband.
